# Strange tacos: breakfast waffle tacos

- Make a waffle,
- fill it with cream and strawberry jam,
- add sliced banana,
- sprinkle cinnamon on top,
- add chocolate sauce,
- fold.

You now have a breakfast waffle taco. Enjoy!
